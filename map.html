Chinese Population by State

About the Data

This map visualizes the Chinese population distribution in the United States using the 2020 American Community Survey (ACS) dataset. The data comes from the B02015 table, specifically the B02015_002E field, which represents the "Chinese Alone" population. It also uses data from the B01003 table, which provides the total population count.

The analysis is performed at three geographic levels: state, county, and census tract. This allows us to explore the concentration of the Chinese population at different scales, from national trends to local communities.
